VIRAT KOHLI

Indian Cricketer

“If You can stay Positive in a Negative Situation, You Win.”

Biography


Virat Kohli was born in Delhi. His date of birth is 5th November 1988. He currently plays for Indian international cricket team. He happens to be a right-handed batsman who bats at the top-order of the team. Because of his aggressive attitude, indefatigable sport spirit and charismatic personality, Kohli is regarded as one of the best players currently playing the game of cricket. He has produced many memorable moments in Cricket history with a consistent performance. He is also said to be the best batsman to be trusted while chasing any target. From the age of three, he has been passionate about the game of cricket. Later, he joined Delhi Cricket Academy and represented Delhi in many regional and national competitions. He also represented India as the captain of the Under-19 team in the World Cup. At the age of 28, he has already scored 38 centuries in the ODIs and 25 centuries in Test Cricket. His hard work was acknowledged by the nation too and he was awarded Padamshri and Arjuna award. His recent achievement includes the pompous victory of the long-awaited Test series against the Kangaroos.
